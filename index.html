Our Story

Veteran Built.
Contractor
Trusted.

Nathan founded 1st Choice Framing in November 2017 as a family and veteran-owned company specializing in custom home framing. In January 2024, the business evolved into 1st Choice General Contractors, Inc. — expanding from framing specialists into a full ground-up general contracting operation.

Whether you need a new home built from scratch, an addition on your existing place, or anything in between — Nathan's crew brings the same discipline they've carried since day one. No shortcuts. No surprises.
